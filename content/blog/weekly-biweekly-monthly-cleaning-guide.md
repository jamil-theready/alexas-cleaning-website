## Weekly vs Bi Weekly vs Monthly Cleaning: Which Is Right for You?

Picking the right cleaning frequency is one of the first decisions you will make when hiring a professional cleaning service. Book too often and you might feel like you are overspending. Book too infrequently and your home never stays as clean as you want it.

There is no single answer that works for everyone. But after years of cleaning homes across Placerville and El Dorado County, Alexa's Cleaning Services can tell you exactly what factors should drive your decision.

---

## Weekly Cleaning: The Gold Standard

Weekly cleaning means your home gets a full professional clean every seven days. It is the most popular option for families and the most effective at maintaining a consistently clean home.

### Who Benefits Most

- **Families with young children.** Kids generate mess at a pace that is hard to keep up with. Crumbs, sticky surfaces, bathroom chaos. Weekly cleaning keeps it from snowballing.
- **Pet owners with two or more animals.** Fur, dander, tracked-in dirt, and the occasional accident. Multiple pets make weekly cleaning a practical necessity.
- **Anyone with allergies or asthma.** Dust, pollen, and dander accumulate fast. Weekly cleaning with HEPA vacuums and proper dusting keeps allergen levels low.
- **Home-based workers.** If you are in your house 40+ hours a week working, the space gets dirtier faster simply because of increased use.

### What to Expect

Weekly cleanings tend to be shorter per visit because there is less buildup between sessions. A 3-bedroom home that takes 3 hours for a deep clean might only take 1.5 to 2 hours for a weekly maintenance visit.

The result is a home that always feels fresh. You never hit that midweek slump where the kitchen floor is sticky and the bathrooms need attention. Everything stays at a baseline level of clean.

### Cost Consideration

Weekly visits have the lowest per-visit cost because each session requires less time and effort. However, the monthly total is higher than biweekly or monthly. For a typical 3-bedroom home in the Placerville area, expect $120 to $160 per visit, or roughly $480 to $640 per month.

---

## Bi Weekly Cleaning: The Sweet Spot

Bi weekly (every two weeks) is our most requested frequency. It balances cost and cleanliness for the majority of households.

### Who Benefits Most

- **Couples without children.** Two adults generate a moderate amount of mess. Every two weeks keeps things in check without overspending.
- **Single-pet households.** One dog or cat produces manageable fur and dander on a biweekly cleaning cycle.
- **People who do light tidying between visits.** If you wipe counters, run the vacuum occasionally, and keep the kitchen reasonable, biweekly professional cleaning fills in the gaps perfectly.
- **Homes under 2,000 square feet.** Smaller spaces are easier to maintain between visits.

### What to Expect

Biweekly visits take a bit longer than weekly because there is more accumulation. Plan for 2 to 3 hours for a standard clean. Bathrooms need more scrubbing. Floors have more dust and debris. Kitchens have more buildup on surfaces.

Most clients report that the first week after cleaning feels great. By the end of the second week, they are ready for the next visit. That rhythm works well for people who can tolerate a slight dip before the next session.

### Cost Consideration

Biweekly visits cost slightly more per visit than weekly because of the additional work, typically $130 to $180 for a 3-bedroom home. Monthly total: $260 to $360.

---

## Monthly Cleaning: The Maintenance Reset

Monthly cleaning is the least frequent option and works best for specific situations.

### Who Benefits Most

- **Single occupants in small spaces.** One person in an apartment or small home generates less mess. Monthly professional cleaning supplements your own routine.
- **Seasonal or vacation homes.** Properties that sit empty most of the month still accumulate dust and need periodic attention to prevent mustiness and pest issues.
- **People who clean regularly themselves.** If you genuinely enjoy cleaning and do it well, monthly professional service handles the deep tasks you skip, like baseboards, inside appliances, and behind furniture.
- **Budget-conscious households.** Monthly is the most affordable entry point into professional cleaning.

### What to Expect

A month of buildup means each visit is essentially a mini deep clean. Plan for 3 to 4 hours. Grout may need scrubbing. Dust accumulation on surfaces will be noticeable. Bathrooms will require more intensive work.

The tradeoff is that your home spends most of the month without professional attention. If you are not maintaining it yourself between visits, the benefits fade quickly.

### Cost Consideration

Monthly visits cost the most per visit because of the increased workload: $150 to $220 for a 3-bedroom home. But the monthly total is the lowest of all three options.

## How to Decide

Ask yourself three questions.

### 1. How Many People and Pets Live in Your Home?

More bodies equals more mess. A household of four with a dog needs more frequent cleaning than a single person in a studio. This is the biggest factor.

### 2. How Much Cleaning Do You Do Yourself?

Be honest. If you run the vacuum and wipe counters regularly, you can stretch to biweekly or monthly. If your own cleaning is limited to loading the dishwasher, weekly service will serve you better.

### 3. What Is Your Budget?

Start with what you can comfortably afford. Monthly is a perfectly valid starting point. You can always increase frequency later. Many of our clients in Placerville start with biweekly and adjust from there based on how they feel.
