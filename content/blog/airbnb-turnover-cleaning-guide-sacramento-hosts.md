## What a Professional Airbnb Turnover Clean Includes

When Sacramento hosts search for **airbnb cleaning services near me**, they need more than a quick wipe-down. A proper turnover clean should cover:

- **Full bathroom sanitization** including toilets, showers, sinks, and mirrors
- **Kitchen reset** with dishes washed, counters wiped, and appliances cleaned inside and out
- **All linens stripped and replaced** with fresh sets
- **Floors vacuumed, mopped, and spot-treated** as needed
- **Trash removed and bins relined**
- **Surfaces dusted and wiped** including nightstands, shelves, and light switches
- **Restocking essentials** like toilet paper, soap, and paper towels

Some hosts also want their cleaners to do a quick staging check. Pillows fluffed. Welcome materials set out. Thermostat adjusted. The best **airbnb cleaning company near me** options will handle all of this as part of the turnover, not as an add-on.

## Common Mistakes Sacramento Hosts Make with Turnover Cleaning

After cleaning hundreds of Airbnb properties, we have seen the same mistakes come up again and again.

**Not having backup supplies on-site.** If your cleaner runs out of toilet paper or dish soap mid-turnover, the job stalls. Keep a storage area stocked with extras so the team can restock without delays.

**Using the wrong products on certain surfaces.** Sacramento rentals often have hardwood floors, granite counters, and stainless steel appliances. Harsh chemicals damage these over time. Make sure your cleaning team uses products that protect your investment.

**Ignoring the smell.** A place can look clean and still smell off. Pet odors from previous guests, cooking smells, musty closets. Professional cleaners address odors, not just visible dirt. That is a detail guests absolutely notice.

**Waiting too long to hire help.** Most hosts call us after they have already burned out. The smart move is to build professional cleaning into your hosting plan from the start, before the bad reviews happen.

## How Often Should You Deep Clean an Airbnb?

Turnover cleans handle the surface-level reset between guests. But every rental also needs periodic [**deep cleaning**](/service/deep-cleaning-services-in-placerville-ca) to stay in top shape. That means pulling out appliances, cleaning behind furniture, washing windows, and scrubbing grout.

For most Sacramento Airbnb properties, a deep clean every four to six weeks works well. High-traffic listings with back-to-back bookings may need it more often. Think of it like maintenance for your car. Turnovers are oil changes. Deep cleans are full service.
